About Keswick on the River

Keswick continues the southwest build-out near Windermere, backing onto the North Saskatchewan River valley. Multi-builder, with single-family and walk-out lots running through the back tier of the community.

Keswick's lot mix means more retaining walls, more terraced patios, and more drainage work than a flat suburban lot — the river-valley back lots especially. ALA handles those properly: engineered base for retaining walls, drainage design, paving-stone patios that sit level on a graded back yard, and fencing tied into the natural slope.

Closest intersection: Anthony Henday Drive & Terwillegar Drive (south)
Local landmarks: North Saskatchewan River valley, Cameron Heights ravine system
Active builders: Multi-builder, premium tier
