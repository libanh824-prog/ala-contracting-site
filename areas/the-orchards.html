About The Orchards at Ellerslie

The Orchards is a 500-acre Brookfield-planned community on the southeast edge of Edmonton, just off Ellerslie Road. The community amenity package — splash park, tennis, ice rink, dog park, community garden — has made it one of the strongest-pulling family neighborhoods in the southeast, with a multi-year build-out runway across multiple builders.

ALA does new-build landscape installs across The Orchards: re-grade, topsoil, sod, paver walkways, full backyard packages, retaining walls on the deeper lots, and fencing on the perimeter. Southeast Edmonton has historically been underserved by premium landscape contractors — most cluster in the southwest. We close that gap with Edmonton-rated workmanship at a fair price.

Closest intersection: Ellerslie Road & 50 Street
Local landmarks: Splash park, tennis courts, ice rink, dog park, community garden, Divine Mercy K–6 School
Active builders: Excel Homes, Brookfield Residential, Coventry, Pacesetter
