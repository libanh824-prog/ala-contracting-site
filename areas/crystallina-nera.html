About Crystallina Nera

Crystallina Nera is a Genstar master-planned community wrapped around Poplar Lake, just south of Anthony Henday off 82 Street. Five active builders are putting up single-family, duplex, and townhome product through 2027, and the new Catholic K–9 school under construction means a strong family demographic moving in over the next several years.

If you've just taken possession of a Crystallina Nera home, your yard is probably bare dirt and clay — sometimes still graded for water flow, sometimes not. ALA handles the full new-build package: re-grading for proper drainage away from the foundation, topsoil, sod or seed, patios, retaining walls where the lot grade requires them, and fencing on perimeter lots. We schedule new-build work spring through fall and pick the winter back up with snow clearing on the same property.
