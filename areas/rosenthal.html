About Rosenthal

Rosenthal is a single-family-heavy new-build community in the Lewis Farms area on Edmonton's west edge, ten minutes from West Edmonton Mall. Construction is still actively underway with Pacesetter and Active Homes building most of the inventory.

If you're closing on a Rosenthal home, expect a yard that needs full new-build treatment: grading away from the foundation, topsoil, sod or seed, walkway, fence, and often a back deck. ALA covers all of that as one quoted package, and we keep the schedule tight enough that a spring possession can have a finished yard by mid-summer.

Local landmarks: West Edmonton Mall (10 minutes), Lewis Farms recreation centre development
