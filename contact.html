Common Questions

FAQ

Do you give free quotes?

Yes. We'll come to the property, assess what the job actually involves, and give you a written quote before anything starts. No charge for that visit.

How far in advance do I need to book?

It depends on the time of year. Spring is busy for landscaping, so we'd recommend reaching out a few weeks ahead. Snow removal contracts are best set up before the season starts (September/October). For urgent jobs, call us directly and we'll tell you what we can do.

Do you do both residential and commercial work?

Yes, we handle both. Sodding and infill landscaping for homeowners, and snow removal and landscape contracts for commercial properties. Project size isn't a barrier.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Edmonton and serve the broader metro area, including St. Albert, Sherwood Park, Spruce Grove, Leduc, Fort Saskatchewan, Beaumont, and surrounding communities. Call us if you're unsure whether you're in our range.

Can I see examples of your past work?

Check our Facebook page for project photos at facebook.com/akurbanscape. We're also happy to walk you through examples during a consultation.

What's the difference between sodding and seeding?

Sod gives you an instant finished lawn: it's faster and less messy but costs more upfront. Seeding is more economical for large areas and produces a very dense, well-rooted lawn, but takes weeks to months to fill in. We'll help you figure out which one makes sense for your project.
